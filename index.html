Why Open Source

We believe open, transparent, and collaborative technology solutions are essential for amplifying indigenous voices and creating sustainable impact.

Transparency & Trust

Open source code ensures indigenous communities control their own data and understand how technology serves their interests.

Community-Driven

Developers, researchers, and community members worldwide can contribute, improve, and adapt solutions for local contexts.

Technical Empowerment

Documentation, knowledge sharing, and open collaboration build local technical capacity and sustainable development in Papua.

Long-term Impact

Open source ensures solutions remain available and maintainable long-term, not dependent on single organizations or funding.
